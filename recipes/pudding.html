Wheat and Hazelnut Pudding

Description

This makes a great breakfast the next day if reheated and served with more honey & butter. cinnamon and raisins can also be added. Source

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of bulgur wheat
  • 8 ounces of hazelnuts – shelled & crushed
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 cups milk or one cup of milk with heavy cream.
  • 1 cup of honey
  • 3 ounces of butter
  • cinnamon and raisins can also be added

Steps

  1. Cook 8 ounces of bulgur wheat according to package directions.
  2. Crush 8 ounces of shelled hazelnuts by placing them in a tea towel and pounding with a hammer. Toast in a medium oven until they start to brown.
  3. Beat three eggs with ½ cup sour cream and 2 cups milk.
  4. Add the bulgur wheat, hazelnuts and 1 cup of honey. Place in a large baking dish.
  5. Slice 3 ounces of butter and place on top of the mixture.
  6. Bake in a medium oven for about 1 hour. If it begins to look dry at any time, add more milk. For a richer dish, replace one cup of milk with heavy cream
